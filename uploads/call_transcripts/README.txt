DROP CALL NOTES & TRANSCRIPTS HERE
====================================

Drop any call notes or transcript (.txt, .md, .pdf, or .docx) from a sales or partner call.

The Inbox Zero Assistant will pick these up automatically on the next run and generate a warm post-call follow-up email in your voice.

Tips:
- Even rough bullet-point notes work — Claude will turn them into a polished email
- Include the prospect's name, company, and email address in the file
- Name the file descriptively: e.g. "call_notes_terabotics_30mar.txt"
- Files are NOT deleted after processing — remove them manually once you've sent the follow-up
